About Me

Jennie Guillot is a front-end web developer and graphic designer with a passion for creating elegant and effective online experiences. Drawing on years of experience in the performing arts as a dancer and aerialist, Jennie brings a keen eye for design and a strong work ethic to every project. Her technical skills include HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript ES6, React, Tailwind CSS, and experience with back-end technologies such as Node and Express. She is adept at responsive design and committed to delivering high-quality, user-centric solutions.
